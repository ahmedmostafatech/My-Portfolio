Origin Story

Started with pixels,
ended up with models.

It started with a simple question: what makes something look good? That curiosity pulled me into graphic design — branding, social media, the craft of making things feel premium. I got obsessed.

Then another question hit harder: what if the tool could think? That's when I discovered machine learning. Computer vision, NLP, deep learning — a whole new way to build things that don't just look intelligent, they actually are.

Today I sit at that intersection. I design brands that communicate clearly, and I build AI systems that process the world intelligently. Two disciplines, one mindset — and I teach both on YouTube in Arabic, because great knowledge should have no language barrier.
